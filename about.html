About Me

Hi, I’m Erica Hunt — a passionate and dedicated Realtor proudly serving the Central Kentucky area for the past three years. Whether you're buying your very first home, looking to upgrade, or searching for the perfect investment property, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. Having raised my wonderful kids in Kentucky, I have a deep love for this region and a strong understanding of its unique neighborhoods, schools, and market trends. My goal is simple: to help my clients feel confident, informed, and supported throughout their real estate journey. I believe in clear communication, honesty, and building relationships that last beyond the closing table. Real estate is more than just a transaction — it’s about helping people turn a big dream into a real address. When I’m not helping clients, you’ll find me enjoying local events, exploring small-town gems, or spending time with my family and fur babies. Let’s find your dream home — together.
